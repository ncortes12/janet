What Can I Offer?

My experience with yoga and the different types has varied, as I have dabbled in many different aspects. I’ve taken workshops in Kundalini, Acro, Vinyasa, Katona, Bikram, Meditation, and so on. I love to create an environment where people feel not only safe, but also supported and can let go of all their troubles and leave feeling calm. I have a passion for learning and will be continuing to study meditation and yoga.

At the moment I have completed my 200-hour teacher training at New Love City in Brooklyn, New York with three incredibly experienced and talented teachers. I plan to begin a mentorship in Yin Yoga soon and hope to continue to explore all the various elements of the practice. At the moment, I live in Greenpoint, Brooklyn and am continuing to grow and move while taking on many different opportunities to teach and bring my knowledge to others.

I am available for parties, corporate events, private sessions, and outdoor classes, as well as studios. Please contact me for more information regarding rates, availability, and specific needs. I’m more than happy to answer any and all questions.
